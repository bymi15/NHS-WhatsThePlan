EVALUATION

The Initial Plan for Term 2


So far we have managed to gather our MoSCoW requirements in the form of Functionality and Usability requirements from our client. Following that, we used User Interface Design principles and Human Computer Interaction concepts to design an effective UI which the client gave positive feedback on.

After which we decided on the Frontend and Backend frameworks that we will use to develop the mobile app through detailed research and proceeded to perform experiments with them. Lastly, we developed a Prototype which we demonstrated to our client and received their approval to go further with development.

Our main plan for the next term involves starting development with Firebase and Ionic to produce the Electronic Care Plan. We aim to complete all the Must and Should Have Requirements to satisfy the client. We will use Trello to facilitate the Agile Scrum Development Methodology which will aid in project management and collaborative processes.

We will utilise two week sprints so that we consistently receive feedback about the application from the client at the end of each sprint. The length of the sprints also helps us get approval regarding development as we will be able to demonstrate a high quality application with each meeting with our client as a result of unit, integration, and user-acceptance testing.
