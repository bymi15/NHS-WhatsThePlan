Experiments

To identify potential limitations and problems in the development in future, we decided to perform some key experiments to test the feasibility of using Firebase and Ionic as our backend and frontend frameworks. Furthermore, we also experimented with user authentication (a vital feature of our app) and a Date Picker Plugin that would help us with one of our requirements. If we are able to successfully integrate the two development frameworks, we can be sure that development in the future will not provide any major obstacles.
